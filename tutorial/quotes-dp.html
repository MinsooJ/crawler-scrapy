From the manufacturer

Oscar Blandi Jasmine Oil

Aromatic Treatment Serum

Sheer brilliance. Banish frizz and impart mirror like shine with this weightless hair oil. A few drops soften, strengthen and seal cuticles, imparting a brilliant veil of luminosity.

Application:

Apply a few drops as needed to towel-dried hair to protect from heat styling. Use on dry hair to calm frizz and enhance shine.

Pro Tip:

Mix a pea size amount of Jasmine Oil with any styling aid in the palm of your hands for an extra shine boost.

Benefit:

  • Releases time-controlled shine to brighten hair
  • Repels humidity for lasting frizz protection
  • Reduces static and flyaways
